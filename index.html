CG Coursework 3D Modelling

I have modelled the Boat Club, Elvet Bridge and the river using WebGL

Here are the controls for manipulating the model

  • Move Camera (Forward, Backward, left and right) - WASD
  • Move Camera (Up and Down) - U/J
  • Rotate model - Arrow Keys and I/K (for all 3 planes of rotation)
  • Moving the boat - B/N (forwards and backwards)
  • Moving the car - C/V (forwards and backwards, also cause the wheels of the car to rotate)
